The deceitfulness of riches and the desires for other things choke out Your Word and grieve Your Spirit in our lives. Forgive us for being led astray from a single-minded, pure, and undistracted devotion to You, our faithful Father, our loving Savior and life-giving Spirit. Pardon us for doubting your promises and questioning your goodness. Send your Spirit to rekindle the fires of devotion in our hearts. For we pray in the name of Jesus Christ our Lord and King. Amen. PRIVATE (Silence is kept for private confession) --- ## The Assurance of Forgiveness Leader: The saying is trustworthy and deserving of full acceptance, that Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners. For you were straying like sheep, but have now returned to the Shepherd and Overseer of your souls. (1 Timothy 1:15; 1 Peter 2:24) All: Thanks be to God who forgives our sins through Jesus Christ, our Lord.
